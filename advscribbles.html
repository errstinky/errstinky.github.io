STEP 1 RULES: The Send Line
  • EV = Send Text to Your Evernote
  • EM = Prevent Text from being sent to your email address (Default = all text is sent to your email address)
  • Additional Email recipients are added by writing their address out in full (no abbreviations).
  • The Send Line is placed at the very TOP of each note.
STEP 2 RULES: The Subject Line
  • # goes before each TAG you want added
  • @ goes before the NOTEBOOK you want your text sent to
  • The Subject Line is placed directly BELOW The Send Line.
