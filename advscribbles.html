ADVANCED SCRIBBLES
Getting the Most out of Scribbly Snap:
  • Send Text to Evernote, Complete with Tags and Notebooks.
  • Send Paper-Based Emails
STEP 1: The Send Line
  • The Send Line Is Placed at the Very TOP of Each Note.
  • EV = Send Text to Your Evernote
  • EM = Prevent Text from Being Sent to Your Email Address (Default = All Text Is Sent to Your Email Address)
  • Additional Email Recipients Are Added by Writing Their Address out in Full (No Abbreviations).
STEP 2: The Subject Line
  • The Subject Line Is Placed Directly BELOW The Send Line.
  • # Goes before Each TAG You Want Added
  • @ Goes before the NOTEBOOK You Want Your Text Sent To
EXAMPLE

The Send Line: EV EM example@gmail.com

The Subject Line: Advanced Scribbles #Cool #Misc @New

  • This Text's Subject Is "Advanced Scribbles"
  • It Would Be Sent to Your Evernote Account And "example@gmail.com".
  • It Would Be Placed in Your "New" NOTEBOOK, with the TAGS "Cool" And "Misc".
  • The Note's Contents Are "Sample text...".
  • *If You Can't Fit Everything on One Line, It's OK to Use Another.
