What excites you most about shopping?
1 Seeing new things and patterns and trends.
2 I get excited when I find something that can help me do what I do better... It doesn't really matter whether or not it's for pleasure or chores; if it provides a significant boost, I love it. For example, when I got my tablet, I was excited because it provided a vastly superior reading experience compared to a paperback.
3 I love, love, love to shop I think it is the excitement of having something new that I find cute.
4 The anticipation of owning something new.
5 Quality, Brand Values and Pricing
6 i most frequently go to shops where there ARE discounts or offers e.g. buy 1 get 1 free OR 30% discount on MRP etc. But in the case of baby food and vegetables I always get fresh vegetables.
7 Being able to use my newly bought items.
8 Looking for good deals. Finding something that I know is worth more than I am paying for it. Especially when it is something that i have been looking for or wanting for a while.
9 The thought of looking cute in whatever I buy.
10 Planning before I go in the store what I need and how quick I can get in and out with dealing with the least amount of human interaction.
11 The idea of going out and looking at what's out there on sale. I love finding good bargains and often, I buy things on sale, even if I wasn't planning to purchases anything while I was out. This is often how I find new stores to shop at.
12 The thrill of getting a good bargain
13 bringing something new and fun into my life
14 being the best at getting a good deal
15 being efficient; getting in and out with little hassle.
