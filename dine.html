Which aspect of dining out annoys you the most?
1 It annoys me the most when people are letting their kids scream or throw fits. Or when parents are totally neglecting their children and they are running all over the restaurant. It is terribly rude.
2 It annoys me to hear other kids crying while I am trying to enjoy my dinner, and yes I have kids.
3 Having to drive after dark to go eat dinner.
4 I get annoyed by the people around me when dining out. I get very annoyed by loud people.
5 when its really busy and i have to wait
6 Usually it's the manners (or lack thereof) of other diners, especially cell phone etiquette and controlling their children. Second would be poor service.
7 rude servers.
8 The only aspect about dining out is having to wait for a table. It doesn't happen all the time but the worst experiences happen on busy weekend evening and there is a 40 minutes wait at your favorite place.
9 People talking on the phone while eating.
10 The uncertainty of who you'll be sitting near. Sometimes a roudy or uncouth table can ruin the whole experience.
11 Waiting for a table
12 Driving to and fro the dining place is the most annoying aspect if I plan to dine out at any point of time. It even becomes a nightmare if I have to drive through a congested road or traffic.
13 Figuring out the tip especially if the waiter/waitress does not do a good job.
14 Annoying customers in the restaurants. Those who are loud, obnoxious, and mean to the staff for no reason at all
15 The noise from other people dining. Sometimes it is so loud I can't hear my dinner companion and this drives me crazy. I can sometimes hear entire conversations from the people sitting behind us in booths.
